About Capital SCO

We are operators who invest

Capital SCO is a UK North-based investment and acquisition firm. Unlike traditional investors, we are hands-on operators who understand the day-to-day realities of running a business and bring genuine operational value beyond capital.

Our approach is founded on integrity, transparency, and a commitment to long-term growth. We treat every acquisition as a partnership - with owners, employees, and communities.

  • Founder-led, operationally experienced team
  • UK-focused
  • Patient capital with no artificial exit pressure
  • Confidential, straightforward deal process
What We Do

Our areas of focus

We work across three key disciplines to identify, acquire, and grow businesses that meet our criteria.

01

Business Acquisition

We acquire established, profitable businesses from owners looking to exit, retire, or move to new chapters. We move swiftly and confidentially from first conversation to completion.

02

Strategic Investment

For owners seeking a growth partner rather than a full exit, we offer minority and majority investment with active operational support and a shared long-term vision.

03

Operational Growth

Post-acquisition, we bring proven frameworks, systems, and operational experience to scale businesses sustainably - protecting their identity while unlocking their full potential.

Our Approach

A process built on clarity

We believe every business sale should be straightforward, respectful, and mutually rewarding. Here is how we work with potential sellers.

01

Initial Enquiry

A confidential, no-obligation conversation. We listen to understand your business, your goals, and your timeline.

02

Evaluation

We review the business efficiently and thoroughly - financials, operations, and market position - with full discretion.

03

Offer & Terms

We present a clear, fair offer with straightforward terms. No hidden conditions, no unnecessary complexity.

04

Completion

We work with your advisors to close efficiently. Post-completion, we ensure a smooth transition for teams and customers alike.
